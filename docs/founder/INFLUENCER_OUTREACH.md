# Influencer Outreach Strategy — eBay Multi-Channel Lister Pro

This document details our plan to partner with micro-influencers (1K–50K followers/subscribers) in the e-commerce reselling niche.

---

## 🎯 Outreach Strategy & Pitch

Micro-influencers have highly engaged communities of active sellers. Instead of paying sponsorship fees, we offer a **value-exchange**:
1. **Lifetime Premium License**: Free access to all Lister Pro updates and feature releases.
2. **Technical Value**: Showing their audience how to run a local-first, zero-dependency utility that replaces $50/month SaaS platforms.
3. **Customization**: Offer to implement a custom watermark overlay layout or specific comp filtering rules for their channel's branding.

---

## 📅 Follow-Up Cadence

- **Day 1**: Initial Outreach (Personalized DM or Email).
- **Day 7**: First Follow-Up (Different angle: highlight Gemini Vision aspect extraction or watermark speed).
- **Day 14**: Final Follow-Up (Close loop: offer one-on-one setup call).
