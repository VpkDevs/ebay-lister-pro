# SEO Content Roadmap — eBay Multi-Channel Lister Pro

This document maps out our 90-day search optimization content clusters, keywords, search volume estimates, and article outlines.

---

## 🔑 Target Keyword Clusters

We target transactional and commercial intent terms used by resellers looking for listing tools:

### Cluster 1: Cross-listing App Comparisons
- **Primary Keyword**: `best crosslisting app 2026` (Est. Volume: 1,200/mo, Difficulty: Low)
- **Secondary Keyword**: `vendoo vs listperfectly` (Est. Volume: 800/mo, Difficulty: Medium)
- **Content Outline**: Complete review of the subscription cost, speed, and limitations of cloud cross-listers. Position Lister Pro as the open-source, local-first alternative.

### Cluster 2: Multi-Channel eBay & Shopify Sync
- **Primary Keyword**: `how to sync ebay to shopify` (Est. Volume: 1,800/mo, Difficulty: Medium)
- **Secondary Keyword**: `ebay shopify inventory sync free` (Est. Volume: 500/mo, Difficulty: Low)
- **Content Outline**: Technical step-by-step guide explaining API sync, webhook setups, and handling double-selling risks. Showcase Lister Pro's automatic archival on sale.

### Cluster 3: VeRO Brand Safety
- **Primary Keyword**: `ebay vero list search` (Est. Volume: 2,400/mo, Difficulty: Low)
- **Secondary Keyword**: `prevent ebay vero suspension` (Est. Volume: 600/mo, Difficulty: Low)
- **Content Outline**: Explanation of eBay's Verified Rights Owner (VeRO) program. Provide a downloadable database checklist. Feature Lister Pro's native case-insensitive check.
