## 💵 Quarterly Estimated Tax Calculations

As a single-member LLC, profits pass through directly to your personal tax return. Since taxes are not withheld automatically from Stripe payouts, you must make quarterly estimated payments using **Form 1040-ES**.

### Safe Harbor Rule
To avoid underpayment penalties, ensure your total estimated payments equal at least:
- **100%** of the tax shown on your previous year's tax return, OR
- **90%** of the tax due for the current year.

### How to Calculate & Pay
1. Track net profit at the end of each quarter (Revenue minus expenses).
2. Calculate estimated federal tax (usually **15% to 25%** depending on personal income bracket).
3. Pay online securely via [IRS Direct Pay](https://www.irs.gov/payments/direct-pay).
