## 🔎 Channel Insights

### 1. Product Hunt (Score: 7.9/10)
- **Fitness**: B2B productivity SaaS, developer tools, and marketplace management solve clear, active pains here.
- **Action**: Ideal for primary launch day (Tuesday) to drive initial feedback, visibility, and product backlinks.

### 2. Reddit (r/flipping, r/ebay) (Score: 7.5/10)
- **Fitness**: Target customers (eBay resellers, flipping side-hustlers) are highly active here. Mod policies are strict against raw marketing, so self-promotion must be framed as a genuine utility storytelling case.
- **Action**: Post story-driven details highlighting the 100% zero-dependency aspect and the self-hosting capability.

### 3. Hacker News (Show HN) (Score: 6.8/10)
- **Fitness**: The zero-dependency core Node.js architecture (no npm modules, raw socket/http parsing, native rate limiters, self-healing db locks) is a highly compelling tech story for HN.
- **Action**: Pitch the technology stack first. HN loves highly optimized, lightweight, zero-dependency implementations.
