## 1. Newsletter Pitch (e.g. for Reseller/Indie Hacker Newsletters)

- **Subject**: A zero-dependency local cross-lister for resellers

```markdown
Hi [Name],

I’ve been reading [Newsletter Name] for a while and love your focus on bootstrapping and side-hustle tools.

I wanted to share a tool I just finished building that solves a major pain point for e-commerce resellers: **eBay Multi-Channel Lister Pro**.

Unlike commercial cross-listing SaaS tools that charge high monthly subscriptions, Lister Pro is a 100% zero-dependency, local-first platform that runs entirely on the user's local machine.

### Why it's interesting:
1. **Zero npm Bloat**: Built entirely on raw Node.js core modules. No Express, no heavy database drivers. Fits in under 10 files.
2. **AI Defect Detection**: Uses Gemini Vision to automatically scan images, isolate scuffs/defects, and extract barcodes.
3. **Double-Sell Shield**: Background watcher auto-archives products on Shopify/Etsy the moment they sell on eBay to protect reseller accounts.

It is completely open-source and free to self-host. I’ve launched it today on Product Hunt and would love to share it with your readers.

Let me know if you'd like a quick screen-share demo or have any questions!

Best regards,
Vincent Kinney
```
