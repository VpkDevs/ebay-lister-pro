# Brand Voice & Archetype — eBay Multi-Channel Lister Pro

This document codifies the brand voice guidelines to keep all marketing, social posts, emails, and platform copy consistent and high-converting.

---

## 🎭 Brand Archetype

Lister Pro aligns with the **Sage** and **Hero** archetypes. We provide deep technical, analytical competence (Sage) to help resellers conquer the bloated, high-fee SaaS landscape (Hero).

### Core Adjectives
1. **Direct**: We say what it does, plain and simple. No fluff.
2. **Technical**: We don't hide the implementation; we celebrate the speed of zero-dependencies and local-first data.
3. **Dry-Witty**: Muted, subtle humor about the state of modern web bloat and expensive subscriptions.
4. **Empowering**: Focus on the merchant's ownership of their data and business.

---

## 🚫 Forbidden Vocab Lexicon

Do not use these generic startup-speak terms:
- **"AI-powered"** / **"AI-driven"**: Overused and lazy. Instead, say: *"Gemini Vision analyses your photos..."*
- **"Revolutionary"** / **"Disruptive"**: Grandiose. Let the zero-dependency architecture speak for itself.
- **"Leverage"**: Corporate filler. Say: *"Uses"* or *"Runs"*.
- **"Synergy"** / **"Paradigm shift"**: Empty buzzwords.

---

## 📝 Sentence Length & Structure

- **Preferred**: Short. Clear. Lots of periods. Direct benefit first.
- **Avoid**: Long, winding sentences with multiple subordinate clauses that bury the point.

### Voice Match Comparison

| ❌ Out-of-Voice (Corporate / Bloated) | ✅ In-Voice (Lister Pro Style) |
|---|---|
| "Our revolutionary AI-powered platform leverages cutting-edge technology to synergetically cross-post listings across major sales channels..." | "Cross-list in 90 seconds. Posts to eBay, Shopify, WooCommerce, and Etsy concurrently. 100% zero external npm dependencies." |
| "Unlock unprecedented efficiency and maximize your bottom line with our next-generation lister..." | "Stop paying $50/month for cross-listing apps that lag. Keep your credentials and listing history local." |

---

## 🎭 Emoji Policy

- **Policy**: Sparingly and purposeful. Never saturate a line with emojis.
- **Allowed Accents**: ⚡ (speed/sync), 👁️ (Gemini Vision/analysis), 🔒 (security/local-first), 🔄 (synchronization), 🟢 (health/live).
- **Prohibited**: 🎉, 🚀, 😱, 💥 (avoid hype-train spam style).
