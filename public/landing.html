Common questions

Got questions? Good.

Do I need to know how to code to set this up?
+
No coding required for day-to-day use. The initial setup requires entering your API keys into a .env file (we provide a step-by-step guide), then you run one command and the dashboard opens in your browser. Everything else is point-and-click.
How accurate is the AI pricing?
+
The AI pulls real recently-sold eBay data for your exact item and condition. You see min, average, and max sold prices on an interactive chart. You always have final say — the Fast / Market / Premium presets let you adjust with one click. Think of it as an instant appraiser, not an autopilot.
What happens if a cross-post to Shopify or Etsy fails?
+
Failed cross-posts are automatically saved to a Dead Letter Queue (DLQ). The system retries them on a regular schedule. You'll see the failure logged in the System Console but your eBay listing publishes regardless. No data is lost, and recovery is fully automatic.
Where is my data stored? Does ListAI see my listings?
+
ListAI Pro runs locally on your own machine. Your listing history, images, and credentials live in files on your computer — not on our servers. Your photos are sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis (under your own API key), but we never store or access your listing data.
Which eBay accounts and regions are supported?
+
ListAI Pro uses the eBay Sell API, which supports all eBay regions. You authenticate with your own eBay developer credentials, so you have full control over which marketplace you post to. US, UK, Canada, Australia, and all eBay markets are supported.
Can I edit the AI-generated listing before publishing?
+
Yes — always. Every field the AI generates is fully editable before you hit Publish. You can change the title, price, description, aspects, shipping options, and which channels to cross-post to. The AI is a first draft, not a final answer.
What's the difference between a listing and a draft?
+
A Draft is saved locally but not yet published to any platform. You can create a draft, come back later, edit it, and then publish. Active listings are live on eBay (and any cross-posted channels). You can end a listing at any time from the History tab.
Can I use this as a service for other sellers?
+
Yes. The Business plan includes multi-user access and a white-label option — you can brand it as your own tool and offer it as a managed listing service. Many VA agencies and consignment shops use exactly this model to charge $2–5 per item for listing services.
