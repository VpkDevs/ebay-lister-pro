Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

Vincent Kinney ("we", "us", or "our") operates eBay Multi-Channel Lister Pro (the "Service"). This policy outlines our data collection, processing, and erasure terms under GDPR and CCPA.

1. Information We Collect

We process only the data necessary to sync and cross-post listings:

  • User Profile: Name, email address, and profile picture from Google Accounts OAuth sign-in.
  • Listing Metadata: Titles, descriptions, condition aspects, pricing comps, and local photo directories.
  • API Access Tokens: Cryptographically handled credentials for eBay, Shopify, WooCommerce, and Etsy.
  • Operational Diagnostics: Uptime logs, endpoint request latencies, and client IP tailing.

2. Third-Party Data Processing

We transmit data to the following platforms to complete operations:

  • Google Gemini: Listing photos and specifications are analyzed by Gemini AI to extract barcodes and suggest categories.
  • Stripe: Processing payment subscriptions.
  • Marketplace APIs: Committing drafts directly to eBay, Shopify, WooCommerce, and Etsy.
  • Temp Image Hosts: Uploading listing photos to tmpfiles.org or file.io fallbacks for remote URL access.

3. GDPR Data Deletion & Erasure

🔒 GDPR RIGHT TO ERASURE: You have the right to request deletion of all your profile, subscription, and inventory history. Since this tool operates locally, you can delete local files under `data/` at any time. To purge your session registry and billing mapping, call `DELETE /api/user/account` or click "Wipe Account" in your profile dashboard.

4. Contact Us

For any privacy requests or GDPR erasure inquiries, email: privacy@ebaylisterpro.local.
